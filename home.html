Beauty in the Kenyan Heritage

The Republic of Kenya is a country in East Africa in the equator , along the Indian Ocean .Kenya borders Ethiopia (north), Somalia (northwestern), Tanzania (south), Uganda and Lake Victoria (west), then South Sudan (northwest). Kenya is a member of the East African Community and of various international structures. The capital is Nairobi . Population has increased significantly in recent years by reaching 49 million. Kenya has several parks where several thousand species of wildlife live. Name of the country is derived from Mount Kenya, which is second in length in Africa. You can visit here to understand more about this beautiful country. Kenya has an amazing heritage from the warm, friendly and hospitable Kenyan people, the beautiful landscapes that is stunningly magnificent from sunrise to sunset, the diverse cultures that bring unique aspects to the nation to the mouth-watering and savoury kenyan dishes.
